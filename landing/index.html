Simple Process

Three steps to a perfect email

No learning curve. Open the app, describe your need, and send — that's the entire workflow.

1

Describe your email

Type a short description — "invoice for website design, Rs. 15,000, due in 7 days" — or upload a PDF document. MailMind AI reads the context automatically.

2

AI detects & generates

The AI classifies the email type (invoice, reminder, proposal, update), picks the right structure, and drafts a professional subject and body in your chosen tone.

3

Review and send

Read the draft, tweak if needed, then send immediately or schedule for a future date. Emails go directly from your connected Gmail account — not a generic sender.
